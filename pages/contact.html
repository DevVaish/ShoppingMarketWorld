Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for all products. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Simply contact our customer service team to initiate a return, and we'll provide you with a prepaid shipping label.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available and takes 2-3 business days. Free shipping is offered on orders over $100. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs fees or import duties.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our website or directly through the carrier's website.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are securely processed and encrypted.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After this window, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Please contact us immediately if you need to make changes.
